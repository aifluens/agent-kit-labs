# Billing

## Plans and pricing

- **Free** — $0/month. Core features with usage limits.
- **Pro** — $49/month. Higher limits, priority support, and team seats.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing with a dedicated account manager.

## Billing cycle

Pro and Enterprise plans are billed monthly on the anniversary of your signup
date. You can switch plans at any time; upgrades are prorated immediately and
downgrades take effect at the start of the next cycle.

## Double charges

If you were charged twice for the same plan in one cycle, this is always an
error. Contact support with both transaction ids and we will refund the
duplicate charge under the standard refund timeline.
