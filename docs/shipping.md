# Shipping

## Delivery times

Standard shipping arrives in 3 to 5 business days. Express shipping arrives in 1
to 2 business days. International orders take 7 to 14 business days and may incur
customs fees.

## Tracking

A tracking number is emailed when your order ships. You can also find it under
Orders in your account dashboard.

## Lost or delayed packages

If a package has not arrived 3 business days past its estimated delivery date,
contact support and we will open a carrier investigation or ship a replacement.
