# Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase. Eligible items are
refunded to the original payment method.

## How long do refunds take?

Once a refund is approved, it is processed within 1 business day on our side.
The funds then take 5 to 7 business days to appear on your card or bank
statement, depending on your bank. For PayPal and store credit, refunds are
usually instant.

## What is eligible

- Unused subscriptions cancelled within 30 days of renewal.
- Defective or incorrect items.
- Duplicate charges (e.g. a double charge for the same plan).

Cancelled orders cannot be refunded again — the original charge was never
captured.
